üîê PAYMENT PROCESSING

Payment Flow 1: Direct Card Entry

üì∏ IMAGE 24: Step 3 with direct card entry
Screenshot: Card Input Form in Step 3

  1. Provider creates prescription in 3-step wizard
  2. Provider enters patient's card information
  3. Authorize.Net processes payment immediately
  4. Payment confirmed ‚Üí Prescription submitted to pharmacy
  5. Queue ID received from pharmacy
  6. Status: "Paid" + "Submitted"
  7. Patient receives confirmation email

Payment Flow 2: Payment Link

  1. Provider creates prescription
  2. Provider selects "Send Payment Link"
  3. Prescription saved as "Pending Payment" (NOT sent to pharmacy yet)
  4. Patient receives email with secure payment link
  5. Patient clicks link and enters card information
  6. Authorize.Net processes payment
  7. Webhook confirms payment received
  8. System automatically submits prescription to pharmacy
  9. Queue ID received
  10. Status updates to "Paid" + "Submitted"
  11. Patient receives confirmation email

Payment Security

  • PCI-DSS Compliant: All payment processing meets PCI-DSS Level 1 requirements
  • No Card Data Stored: Platform never stores raw credit card numbers or CVV codes
  • Tokenized Transactions: Authorize.Net handles all sensitive payment data
  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All payment pages use HTTPS encryption
